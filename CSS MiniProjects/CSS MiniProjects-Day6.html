Mini Project:7 - About Us Section

Shree's Boutique

Welcome to Shree's Boutique!

Our journey began in 2016 with our first stall in Chennai, India. After the success of this humble beginning, Thanvi Boutique launched its boutique shop in 2018 and has never looked back. Today, with three branches across Tamil Nadu—in Chennai, Madurai, and Karaikudi—we have become the world’s preferred choice for handloom sarees, setting new standards of customer delight every day. Today we have our own handlooms and a tailoring section to curate the needs of our customers.

Our Mission

At Shree's Boutique, we are driven by a passion to empower individuals with timeless elegance while preserving the traditions woven into each fabric. By showcasing the skill and dedication of our talented artisans and weavers, we contribute to sustaining age-old crafts, supporting local communities, and fostering a sense of pride.
